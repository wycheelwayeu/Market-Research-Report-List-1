<p><p>Silica gel desiccant packets are classified into three main types based on their indicators and moisture absorption properties. Silica gel white is the basic type, effectively absorbing moisture without indicating saturation. Silica gel blue contains cobalt chloride, which changes from blue to pink when saturated, providing a clear visual cue. Silica gel orange uses a non-toxic indicator that shifts from orange to green when absorbing moisture. These types cater to various applications, including food preservation, electronics protection, and industrial uses.</p></p>
