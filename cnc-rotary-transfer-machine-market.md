<p><p>CNC rotary transfer machines are categorized into vertical axis and horizontal axis types, each designed for efficient multi-station machining processes. Vertical axis rotary transfer machines utilize vertical tooling arrangements, facilitating compact layouts and optimizing upward workpiece handling, ideal for smaller parts. In contrast, horizontal axis rotary transfer machines offer a more extended workspace, allowing for easier loading and unloading of larger components, enhancing accessibility and efficiency in mass production settings. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs, driving productivity and precision.</p></p>
