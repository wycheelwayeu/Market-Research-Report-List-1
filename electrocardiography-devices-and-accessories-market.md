<p><strong>The Electrocardiography Devices and Accessories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Track</li><li>Three Ways</li><li>Six Ways</li><li>Twelve Ways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrocardiography devices and accessories are categorized by the number of leads they utilize to monitor heart activity. Single-track devices measure a single channel, offering basic heart rate information. Three-way devices provide a more detailed view of the heart's electrical activity through three channels. Six-way devices enhance this further by capturing six channels, allowing for improved diagnostic capability. Finally, twelve-way devices deliver comprehensive data across twelve leads, enabling detailed assessment of cardiac conditions and enhanced clinical insights for healthcare providers.</p></p>
