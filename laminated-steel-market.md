<p><strong>The Laminated Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusion Method Laminated Steel</li><li>Bonding Agent Laminated Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated steel market is categorized into two main types: Fusion Method Laminated Steel and Bonding Agent Laminated Steel. </p><p>Fusion Method Laminated Steel involves the melting and fusing of steel layers under high temperatures to create a homogeneous material, offering enhanced strength and durability. </p><p>In contrast, Bonding Agent Laminated Steel utilizes adhesives or other bonding agents to join steel layers, providing flexibility in design and potentially reducing production costs. Both methods serve various industrial applications, enhancing performance and material properties.</p></p>
