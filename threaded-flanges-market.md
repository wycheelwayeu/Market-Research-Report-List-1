<p><strong>The Threaded Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread</li><li>External Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The threaded flanges market can be categorized into internal thread and external thread types. Internal threaded flanges feature threads on the inside, allowing for secure attachment to pipes with corresponding external threads, making them ideal for tight spaces. Conversely, external threaded flanges have threads on the outside, facilitating connections to internal-threaded fittings. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing, as they provide reliable connections that withstand high pressure and fluctuating temperatures.</p></p>
