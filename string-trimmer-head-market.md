<p><p>The string trimmer head market features three main types: fixed head, automatic feed, and bump feed. Fixed head trimmers use pre-cut lines that require manual replacement, offering simplicity and durability. Automatic feed models dispense line as needed, providing convenience for uninterrupted work. Bump feed heads allow users to extend the line by tapping the trimmer's head on the ground, striking a balance between control and ease. Each type caters to different user preferences and trimming requirements in landscaping.</p></p>
