<p><ul><li>Active Components</li><li>Passive Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical components market can be divided into two main categories: active and passive components. Active components, such as transistors, diodes, and integrated circuits, require an external power source to operate and can amplify signals or produce energy. In contrast, passive components, like resistors, capacitors, and inductors, do not require power to function; they simply store or dissipate energy. Both types are essential for various applications, including consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and industrial equipment, driving innovation and technological advancement.</p></p>
