<p><p>Liquid nitrogen traps are essential components used in cryogenic systems to capture and condense gases. The market is divided into two main types: inline traps and right-angle traps. Inline traps are designed to fit seamlessly within existing gas lines, ensuring minimal disruption and maintaining efficient flow. In contrast, right-angle traps facilitate the bending of gas pathways, allowing for space optimization in various setups. Both types play a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and safety of cryogenic applications.</p></p>
