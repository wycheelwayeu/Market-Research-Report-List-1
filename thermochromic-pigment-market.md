<p><ul><li>Reversible Thermochromic Pigment</li><li>Irreversible Thermochromic Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermochromic pigment market is primarily divided into two types: reversible and irreversible thermochromic pigments. Reversible thermochromic pigments change color in response to temperature fluctuations and revert to their original hue upon cooling, making them ideal for applications like mood rings and temperature-sensitive labels. In contrast, irreversible thermochromic pigments permanently change color when exposed to heat, making them suitable for applications like certain security features and novelty items where a lasting change is desired.</p></p>
