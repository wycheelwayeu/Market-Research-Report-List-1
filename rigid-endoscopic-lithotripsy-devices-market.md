<p><p>The rigid endoscopic lithotripsy devices market comprises several types, including ultrasonic lithotripsy and ballistic lithotripsy. Ultrasonic lithotripsy utilizes high-frequency sound waves to fragment kidney stones, ensuring minimal thermal damage to surrounding tissues. Ballistic lithotripsy employs mechanical energy to break stones into smaller fragments, often using a mounted probe for precise targeting. Other types may encompass laser lithotripsy and pneumatic devices, each offering distinct methodologies for stone management, enhancing efficiency and patient outcomes in urological procedures.</p></p>
