<p><p>The market for soft capsule filling machines is categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic machines. Manual capsule filling machines require human intervention for the entire process, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines streamline some aspects, reducing labor while still necessitating operator input, ideal for medium-scale production. Fully automatic capsule filling machines operate independently, optimizing efficiency and speed, making them the preferred choice for large-scale manufacturing by minimizing human labor and maximizing output.</p></p>
