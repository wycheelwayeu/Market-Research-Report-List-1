<p><p>The Polyethersulfone (PES) dialyzer market is segmented into high permeability and low permeability types based on their membrane characteristics. High permeability dialyzers facilitate enhanced solute clearance, making them ideal for patients requiring efficient dialysis treatment. They allow larger molecules to pass through more readily. Conversely, low permeability dialyzers are designed for controlled solute removal, suitable for patients with specific needs where excessive clearance could be detrimental. The choice between them impacts treatment effectiveness and patient outcomes.</p></p>
