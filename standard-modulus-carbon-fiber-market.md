<p><p>Standard modulus carbon fiber is categorized based on the number of filaments per tow, influencing its strength and application. The 1K type features a single thousand filaments, ideal for lightweight applications. The 3K has 3,000 filaments, suitable for more demanding structures. The 6K offers increased strength for industrial use, while the 12K supports heavy-duty applications. Fibers categorized as "Above 12K" are designed for specialized applications requiring superior durability and structural integrity, catering to high-performance industries.</p></p>
