<p><p>ZigBee remotes operate primarily in three frequency bands: 2.4 GHz, 900 MHz, and 868 MHz. The 2.4 GHz band is the most widely used globally, offering high data rates and interoperability, but is subject to interference from Wi-Fi and other devices. The 900 MHz band is prevalent in North America, providing longer range and better penetration through obstacles, making it suitable for expansive applications. The 868 MHz band is mainly used in Europe, focusing on lower power consumption and regulatory compliance in industrial and residential settings.</p></p>
