<p><p>The uncooled infrared detector market consists of various types, each serving specific applications. Bolometers measure infrared radiation by detecting temperature changes in a material. Pyroelectric detectors sense temperature variations from moving thermal sources. Thermopile detectors convert thermal energy into electrical signals for accurate measurements. Diode detectors exploit semiconductor properties to detect infrared light. Thermal capacitance detectors measure temperature changes with high sensitivity. Other market types may include hybrid and advanced technologies that enhance performance in diverse applications, from security to environmental monitoring.</p></p>
