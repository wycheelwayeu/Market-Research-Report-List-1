<p><strong>The Audiometric Screening Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OAE Screening Equipment</li><li>ABR Screening Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audiometric screening equipment encompasses various technologies used to assess hearing ability. Otoacoustic Emission (OAE) screening equipment measures sound waves produced in the inner ear, helping detect hearing dysfunction, especially in newborns. Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR) screening equipment evaluates neural response to sound stimuli, making it essential for diagnosing auditory processing issues in both infants and adults. Together, these technologies enhance early detection and intervention for hearing impairments, promoting better auditory health outcomes across diverse populations.</p></p>
