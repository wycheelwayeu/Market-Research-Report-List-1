<p><strong>The Polyurethane Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Organic Metal Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane catalyst market includes three primary types: reactive amine catalysts, non-reactive amine catalysts, and organic metal catalysts. Reactive amine catalysts participate in the chemical reaction, enhancing polymer formation and properties. Non-reactive amine catalysts accelerate the curing process without becoming part of the final product, offering efficiency in production. Organic metal catalysts, typically based on tin, facilitate rapid curing and enhance product stability, making them essential for various polyurethane applications in industries like automotive, construction, and furniture.</p></p>
