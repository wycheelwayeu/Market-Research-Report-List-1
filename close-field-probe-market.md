<p><p>The Close Field Probe market is segmented into passive and active probes, each serving distinct purposes in measuring electromagnetic fields. In contrast, Active Close Field Probes are powered devices that amplify signal detection, enabling real-time analysis and dynamic measurements. Both types are crucial for ensuring compliance with electromagnetic safety standards in various industries, including telecommunications and electronics.</p></p>
