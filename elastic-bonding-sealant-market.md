<p><p>The elastic bonding sealant market comprises three primary types: rubber-based, resin-based, and oil-based sealants. Rubber-type sealants offer excellent flexibility and weather resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Resin-type sealants provide strong adhesion and durability, often used in construction and automotive industries. Oil-based sealants deliver good moisture resistance and bonding capabilities, primarily utilized in industrial settings. Each type is chosen based on specific adhesion requirements, environmental conditions, and performance characteristics needed for various applications.</p></p>
