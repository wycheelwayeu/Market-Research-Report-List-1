<p><p>The multi-band booster market is categorized into different types based on the number of frequency bands they support. Dual-band boosters operate on two distinct frequencies, often for 4G and 5G. Tri-band boosters enhance three frequency bands, improving coverage significantly. Quad-band boosters extend support to four bands, catering to diverse cellular networks. Five-band boosters offer the most extensive coverage, encompassing five frequency bands, ideal for complex environments with multiple network demands, providing comprehensive signal enhancement for users.</p></p>
