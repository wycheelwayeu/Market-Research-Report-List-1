<p><p>The baking machinery market comprises several types, including rack ovens, deck ovens, convection ovens, and others. Rack ovens are designed for large-scale baking, allowing multiple trays to be baked simultaneously. Deck ovens provide consistent heat from multiple layers, ideal for artisan bread and pastries. Convection ovens utilize fans to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and quicker cooking times. Other types may include rotary ovens, pizza ovens, and specialized equipment, catering to diverse baking needs across the food service industry.</p></p>
