<p><strong>The Civil Aircraft Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The civil aircraft tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature cord layers arranged perpendicular to the tread, providing better performance, fuel efficiency, and longevity, making them popular for modern aircraft. In contrast, bias tires have cord layers arranged at angles, offering better stability and durability under heavy loads but with less fuel efficiency. Both types serve specific aircraft requirements, depending on operational needs and design considerations.</p></p>
