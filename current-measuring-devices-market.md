<p><p>The current measuring devices market includes several types, notably clamp ammeters, probe ammeters, and fork ammeters. Clamp ammeters enable users to measure electrical current without disconnecting cables, making them ideal for quick assessments. Probe ammeters are designed for direct contact with circuits, often providing precise readings in compact or hard-to-reach areas. Fork ammeters combine features of clamp and probe types, allowing for measurements in confined spaces while maintaining accuracy. Collectively, these tools serve various applications in electrical diagnostics and maintenance.</p></p>
