<p><p>The tape storage market comprises several types, including helical scan tape drives, minicartridges, and data cartridges. Helical scan tape drives use a unique recording method that allows for high data density and efficient read/write operations, often utilized in video and audio recording. Minicartridges are compact tape storage solutions designed for smaller data requirements, while data cartridges are larger, providing extensive storage for enterprise-level applications, such as backups and archiving. Each type serves distinct needs within the broader data management landscape.</p></p>
