<p><p>Stainless steel powder for powder metallurgy is categorized into three main grades: Austenitic, Martensitic, and Ferritic. </p><p>Austenitic grades offer excellent corrosion resistance and formability, making them ideal for applications requiring high ductility. Martensitic grades provide enhanced strength and wear resistance, suitable for tooling and high-stress components. Ferritic grades, characterized by good corrosion resistance and magnetic properties, are often used in automotive and industrial applications. Each grade serves distinct market needs based on performance characteristics and intended uses.</p></p>
