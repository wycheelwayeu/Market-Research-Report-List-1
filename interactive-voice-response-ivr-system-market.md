<p><p>The Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system market is primarily divided into speech-based and touch-tone based categories. Speech-based IVR systems allow users to interact using voice commands, enabling natural and intuitive communication. In contrast, touch-tone based systems rely on users pressing keys on their phone's keypad to navigate menus. Both types serve to enhance customer service efficiency by automating responses, streamlining call handling, and providing information, but they cater to different user preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
