<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The front illuminated CMOS sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS). PPS utilizes a simpler architecture, offering lower image quality and speed, making it cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, APS incorporates individual amplifiers for each pixel, enhancing image quality and performance, especially in dynamic scenes. This technology is commonly used in high-resolution cameras and smartphones, driving advancements in imaging, low-light performance, and overall user experience.</p></p>
