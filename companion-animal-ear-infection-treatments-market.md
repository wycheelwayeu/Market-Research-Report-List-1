<p><p>The Companion Animal Ear Infection Treatments Market is categorized into three primary types: Otitis Externa, Otitis Media, and Otitis Interna. Otitis Externa affects the external ear canal, commonly caused by allergies or infections, necessitating topical treatments like ointments or drops. Otitis Media involves the middle ear, often requiring systemic antibiotics and analgesics. Otitis Interna affects the inner ear, potentially impacting balance and hearing, typically treated with more aggressive medications or surgery due to its complexity and severity.</p></p>
