<p><ul><li>Elastomeric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Firestop sealants are essential for preventing the spread of fire, smoke, and harmful gases in building structures. The market is primarily categorized into two types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and provide a durable seal that can withstand temperature fluctuations and building movement. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, forming an insulating layer that effectively blocks fire and smoke. Both types are critical for ensuring fire safety and compliance in construction.</p></p>
