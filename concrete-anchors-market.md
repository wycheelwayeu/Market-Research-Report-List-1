<p><p>The concrete anchors market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical concrete anchors and adhesive concrete anchors. Mechanical concrete anchors rely on physical expansion or interlocking mechanisms to secure loads, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, adhesive concrete anchors utilize strong bonding agents to adhere to the substrate, providing superior durability in environments with moisture or vibration. Each type serves specific installation needs, offering varying levels of strength, ease of installation, and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
