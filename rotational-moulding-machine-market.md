This technique involves heating a plastic powder in a mould that is rotated along two perpendicular axes, allowing the material to coat the inside of the mould evenly. <p><strong>The Rotational Moulding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biaxial Rotation Type Rotational Moulding Machine</li><li>Rock and Roll Type Rotational Moulding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotational moulding machine market features two primary types: biaxial rotation and rock and roll. Biaxial rotation type machines utilize dual-axis movement, allowing for even material distribution and uniform wall thickness in moulds, enhancing product quality. In contrast, rock and roll machines involve a simpler rocking motion during the heating and cooling process, making them more cost-effective for smaller production runs. Both types cater to diverse applications, including toys, tanks, and automotive components, optimizing production efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
